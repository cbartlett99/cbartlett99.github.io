Why is TikTok Such a Drug?

Recently, the app TikTok has shot up in popularity and use. I, like many others, became unashamedly addicted to TikTok. Watching the looping videos of comedy, dance, and even cooking has now become part of my daily routine. With all of this in mind, it begs the question, why is it so addicting?

Communication Conundrums: Netflix's Censorship

Have you ever wondered why some countries allow some shows, but ban others? Or, how Netflix isn't a universal site for everyone to watch the same thing? I can't tell you how many times I tried to watch a show only to find out it's only in Australia instead. In this podcast, my groupmembers and I discuss the implications Netflix had to undergo as a watching site in certain countries based on the censorship focuses within them.
